General Maintenance

Whether it is a one off, or a regular arrangement - the team at BGS enjoy stepping in and doing the work to ensure your garden is looking cared for, and in tip-top shape regardless of the season.

Examples include mowing your lawn regularly, removing weeds from grass and flowerbeds, rake and remove decomposing leafs, trim and prune ivy and wisteria that can damage building and shaping hedges to prevent them from encroaching on your space.

To prevent your garden from becoming unkempt and overgrown, we can set up a schedule with you that we stay on top off (no more reminders) to keep those gardening chores happening regularly.

Patios

Concreate, Clay, Tile, Flagstone – you name your style and Will and the team at BGS can lay it. We understand that patios can be quite the investment for homeowners, so we will be on hand to discuss options and provide expert opinion to ensure you choose a patio that lasts years to come.

We also offer a patio cleaning service. Weather and footfall can take its toll, plus patios easily become home to moss, lichen and algae which can be a slip risk or potentially cause structural damage if not kept on top of. To remove dirt, stains and plant growth the team use an economical high-pressure cold water jet system that uses less water than a hosepipe.
